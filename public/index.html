FAQ

Common Questions

Is AquaRythu really free? expand_more
Yes. Feed logging, pond tracking, DOC management, and full feed history are free forever with no time limits.
Does it work without internet at the pond? expand_more
Yes — you can log feed rounds and tray observations offline at the pond side. Data syncs automatically when you reconnect.
Can I manage multiple ponds & farms? expand_more
Absolutely. The app supports unlimited ponds and farms from a single dashboard. Great for farmers managing 5–20+ acres.
Which Android devices are supported? expand_more
Any Android smartphone running Android 8.0 or higher. Works on budget devices commonly used in the field.
How does smart feeding work? expand_more
Feed recommendations are calculated using ABW, biomass, tray check results, and historical FCR — giving you the right quantity for each round, each day.
Can workers or supervisors use the app? expand_more
Yes. Add workers with limited permissions for feed logging only, and supervisors with broader access to monitor multiple ponds.
